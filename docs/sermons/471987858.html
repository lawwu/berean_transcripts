Believe in Jesus, the Messiah of God, and then in whatever wilderness the Lord ordained, big or small, long or short, trust and obey.

Trust and obey, for there's no other way to be what? To have life. To have zoway, life to the full. You cannot save yourself. Praise God that he is more than willing to save, and praise God that he is leading us through every and any wilderness that we face. Amen? Not because we are good, not because we are able to obey, but because he is good. And he did obey. He was the perfect spotless lamb of God. He is good. Wilderness tests are on purpose. They are for testing, they are for training, but he will not leave us there. They will end, and we will be in glory at home with the Lord forever and ever and ever.

What a glorious hope Christians have. No matter what election results, no matter what political environments, no matter what kind of persecution, oppression, and strife we experience, he is coming back, and he will take us home.
