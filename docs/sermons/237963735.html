Do you also believe when he says, "Broad is the way that leads to destruction." And many will be found on it, but narrow is the path that leads to eternal life. And very few will be found on it. Do you also believe that? Do you also apply that in your life? As we come before the Lord and we consider God's patience and severity with his people, and how all through all of this, our God is completely sovereign, that his word has not failed us, that his gift and his calling are irrevocable, that as we take some time to consider all of these things, again, not to simply superficially say, "I believe it," but to examine and honestly pray before God, "I believe, help my unbelief," so that our faith will be real.
